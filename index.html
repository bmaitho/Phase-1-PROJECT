THE TOP 5

Eiffel tower ,Paris

Paris, the City of Lights, is known for its romantic atmosphere, iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, and its delicious food scene.

Burj Khalifa ,Dubai

Dubai, a city in the United Arab Emirates, is known for its luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building, dominates the skyline.

Soneva Fush ,Maldives

The Maldives is a South Asian country in the Indian Ocean known for its many islands and beautiful beaches. Its luxurious resorts provide overwater bungalows and private beaches.

Colosseum, Rome

Rome, the "Eternal City", is a historic city in Italy, known for its ancient ruins, world-class museums, and delicious food. The Colosseum, a massive amphitheater, is a symbol of the Roman Empire.
